# Scenario 2: Submitting Emergency Medical Request

**Persona**: John Kamau (Group Member)  
**Goal**: Request emergency funds for mother's hospitalization  
**Context**: Wednesday evening, crisis situation  
**Duration**: ~5 minutes  
**Emotional State**: Stressed, urgent need

---

## Background

John's mother collapsed and was rushed to Kenyatta National Hospital. The doctors say she needs immediate surgery costing 15,000 KES. John has only 3,000 KES saved. He remembers the Chama group and decides to request emergency help.

---

### 1. The Crisis
- **Time**: Wednesday, 6:45 PM
- **Location**: Hospital waiting area
- John's hands are shaking as he holds his phone
- Doctor says: "We need 15,000 KES deposit for surgery tonight"
- **Thought**: "The Chama... I can request help there"
- Opens Kijiji-Trust app

- **Time**: 6:46 PM

## Design Validation

### HCI Principles Demonstrated:

1. **Error Prevention**: 
   - Form validation prevented mistakes
   - Amount formatted automatically
   - Clear field labels

2. **Visibility**:
   - Big "Request Help" button
   - Icons made options clear
   - Status timeline visible

3. **Feedback**:
   - Immediate confirmation
   - Real-time approval notification
   - M-Pesa integration visible

4. **Simplicity**:
   - Only 4 required fields
   - No complex navigation
   - One clear path to goal

5. **Accessibility**:
   - Works under stress
   - Icons supplement text
   - Large touch targets
   - Fast loading

6. **Human-Centered**:
   - Direct call option (human connection)
   - Empathetic language
   - Quick process respects urgency
