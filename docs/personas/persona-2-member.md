## Background & Context

John has been a boda boda driver for 8 years. His income varies daily, but he tries to save regularly. He joined a local Chama 2 years ago to have a safety net for emergencies and to help his family.

He often misses physical meetings because of his irregular work schedule. He worries about being left out of important decisions and not knowing if his contributions are properly recorded.

---

## Goals & Motivations

### Primary Goals:
1. **Track His Contributions**: Know exactly what he's paid and what he owes
2. **Access Emergency Funds**: Get help quickly when family needs it
3. **Stay Informed**: Know what's happening without attending every meeting
4. **Build Security**: Create a financial cushion for his family

### Secondary Goals:
- Understand how group money is being used
- Participate in group decisions when possible
- Prove his regular contributions to build trust
- Help other members when they need it

---

## Pain Points & Frustrations

### Current Challenges:
1. **Missing Meetings**
   - "My work hours are unpredictable, I can't always attend"
   - Feels out of the loop when he misses meetings
   - Worried others think he's not committed

2. **Record Uncertainty**
   - "Did my last payment get recorded properly?"
   - No way to verify his contribution history
   - Anxious about disputes over payments

3. **Emergency Access**
   - "When crisis hits, I need help NOW, not next week"
   - Current process takes too long
   - Embarrassing to call admin repeatedly to check status

4. **Trust Concerns**
   - Can't see where group money goes
   - Worries about transparency
   - No proof of his participation

## Needs from Kijiji-Trust

### Must-Haves:
1. **Simple Login**: Quick access with phone number
2. **Clear Status View**: "Am I paid up? Do I owe anything?"
3. **Easy Request Submission**: Submit emergency request in under 2 minutes
4. **Mobile Notifications**: Know when something important happens
5. **Low Data Usage**: App shouldn't eat up his data bundle

### Nice-to-Haves:
- See other members' contributions (for transparency)
- Download his contribution history
- Vote on group decisions from phone
- Chat with group members

---

## Design Implications

### For John, the system must:
1. ✅ **Be extremely simple** - Maximum 3 clicks for any task
2. ✅ **Work on basic smartphones** - Low memory, small screen
3. ✅ **Load quickly on 3G** - Optimize images and data
4. ✅ **Use visual indicators** - Icons + colors, not just text
5. ✅ **Have large buttons** - Easy to tap while on the move
6. ✅ **Show clear status** - "Paid" vs "Pending" immediately visible
7. ✅ **Confirm actions** - "Payment recorded successfully!"
8. ✅ **Support offline viewing** - Check status without internet
