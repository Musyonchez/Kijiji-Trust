## Background & Context

Mary works as an accountant and was chosen as treasurer for her church Chama because of her financial expertise. She manages records for 30 members and takes her role very seriously.

She currently uses Excel for tracking but finds it time-consuming to manually update and cross-reference physical receipts with digital records. She wants a system that makes her job easier and more accurate.

---

## Goals & Motivations

### Primary Goals:
1. **Accurate Record-Keeping**: Zero errors in financial tracking
2. **Easy Reconciliation**: Match contributions with actual funds
3. **Generate Reports**: Create monthly summaries for group
4. **Transparency**: Show members exactly where money goes
5. **Efficiency**: Spend less time on manual data entry

### Secondary Goals:
- Identify payment patterns
- Predict group cash flow
- Alert members about upcoming dues
- Archive historical data properly

---

## Pain Points & Frustrations

### Current Challenges:
1. **Manual Data Entry**
   - "I spend 3-4 hours every month updating spreadsheets"
   - Risk of typos and calculation errors
   - Double work (physical notebook + Excel)

2. **Reconciliation Nightmares**
   - Hard to match verbal payments with records
   - Some members claim they paid but no record exists
   - Time-consuming to verify each transaction

3. **Reporting Burden**
   - Creating monthly reports manually
   - Members want different views of the same data
   - Hard to visualize trends and patterns

4. **No Audit Trail**
   - Can't prove who made changes to records
   - Disputes about past transactions
   - No backup if Excel file corrupts

## Design Implications

### For Mary, the system must:
1. ✅ **Be accurate and reliable** - No calculation errors ever
2. ✅ **Work on desktop and mobile** - Flexibility in data entry
3. ✅ **Export data easily** - CSV, Excel, PDF formats
4. ✅ **Show detailed transaction history** - Filter, sort, search
5. ✅ **Provide audit logs** - Who, what, when for every action
6. ✅ **Support keyboard shortcuts** - Faster data entry
7. ✅ **Have bulk actions** - Verify 10 payments at once
8. ✅ **Include visual analytics** - Charts showing trends
