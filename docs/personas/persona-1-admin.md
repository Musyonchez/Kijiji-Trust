# Persona 1: Grace Wanjiku - Group Administrator

![Persona Type: Primary User - Admin]

---

## Basic Information

**Name**: Grace Wanjiku  
**Age**: 42 years old  
**Location**: Nairobi, Kenya  
**Occupation**: Small Business Owner (Vegetable Market Vendor)  
**Education**: Secondary School Certificate  
**Tech Proficiency**: Moderate (Uses WhatsApp, M-Pesa daily)  
**Income Level**: Middle class  

---

## Background & Context

Grace has been running a vegetable stall at the local market for 15 years. She is well-respected in her community and currently leads a women's savings group (Chama) of 25 members, mostly fellow market vendors and neighbors. 

The group has been meeting monthly for 3 years, pooling money for emergencies and small business investments. Currently, Grace tracks everything in a physical notebook and Excel spreadsheet, which has led to disputes and confusion.

---

## Goals & Motivations

### Primary Goals:
1. **Maintain Trust**: Keep the group transparent and trustworthy
2. **Reduce Administrative Burden**: Spend less time on record-keeping
3. **Reach More Members**: Enable participation from those who can't attend physical meetings
4. **Quick Emergency Response**: Help members faster when crisis strikes

### Secondary Goals:
- Reduce errors in record-keeping
- Have digital backup of all transactions
- Generate reports easily for group meetings
- Grow the group to include more community members

---

## Pain Points & Frustrations

### Current Challenges:
1. **Manual Record-Keeping**
   - "I spend hours updating my notebook after each meeting"
   - Excel spreadsheet often has calculation errors
   - No backup if notebook is lost or damaged

2. **Communication Delays**
   - Hard to notify all 25 members quickly
   - Some members miss meetings and fall out of the loop
   - Emergency requests take too long to communicate

3. **Trust Issues**
   - Some members doubt the accuracy of records
   - No way to independently verify transactions
   - Disputes arise about who paid and when

4. **Geographic Limitations**
   - Some women live far and can't attend every meeting
   - Time-consuming to collect contributions in person
   - Can't help members who have relocated

## Needs from Kijiji-Trust

### Must-Haves:
1. **Simple Setup**: Create a group in under 10 minutes
2. **Easy Invitations**: Share group code via WhatsApp
3. **Clear Dashboard**: See group balance at a glance
4. **Quick Approvals**: Approve emergency requests with 2-3 taps
5. **Mobile-Friendly**: Works well on smartphone with limited data

### Nice-to-Haves:
- Export reports for meetings
- Send reminders for contributions
- Track member participation
- See group growth over time

---

## Design Implications

### For Grace, the system must:
1. ✅ **Use clear, simple language** - No technical jargon
2. ✅ **Have large, touch-friendly buttons** - Easy to tap on phone
3. ✅ **Show confirmation for important actions** - "Are you sure you want to approve 10,000 KES?"
4. ✅ **Work on 3G/4G** - Lightweight, fast loading
5. ✅ **Provide visual status indicators** - Green = paid, Yellow = pending, Red = overdue
6. ✅ **Enable WhatsApp integration** - Share invite codes easily
7. ✅ **Show clear member list** - Who's in the group, who paid
8. ✅ **Have offline capability** - View data even without internet

## Success Metrics

Grace will consider Kijiji-Trust successful if:
- ✅ All 25 current members join within first month
